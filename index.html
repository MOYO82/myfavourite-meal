My favourite meal is jollof rice and fried chicken with plantain

Rice is an edible starchy cereal grain of a grass plant belonging to the family Poaceae. It is considered a staple food that is believed to have originated in southern India and is widely eaten in many countries around the world.
Jollof rice dish from West Africa. The dish is typically made with long-grain rice, although its ingredients and preparation methods vary across different regions. TJollof rice originated in the Senegambian region, which included parts of today's Senegal, The Gambia, and Mauritania

Chicken is the most widely domesticated fowl on earth. It is a gregarious bird that is extensively used for its meat and eggs. Chicken is believed to be descended from the wild Indian and Southeast Asian red junglefowl. It is reported to be rich in proteins. Tryptophan, an amino acid found in chicken, has been linked to higher levels of serotonin in our brains.

Ingredients Measurements
Rice 6 cups of rice
Chicken 1 full chicken
Tomatoes 1 large size tomatoe
Blended pepper sauce 2 cups
Onion 1 medium bulb
Garlic 4 clove
Bay leaves 2 leaves
Thyme 2 tablespoons
Curry powder 1 tablespoon
White pepper 1 tablespoon
Salt 1 tablespoon
Chicken bouillion powder 1 tablespoon
Butter 2 tablespoon
Red bell pepper 3 pieces
scotch bonnet 2 pieces
Vegetable oil 3/4 cup

Methods to prepare

  • Heat vegetable oil in a cooking pan add diced onions and cook for 3-5 minutes
  • Add the totamo paste and fry for 5 minutes
  • Add the garlic, ginger and bay leaves and cook for 2 minutes
  • Add the blended pepper allow to cook for about 10-15minutes
  • Season with thyme, curry powder, salt, white pepper and ckicken bouillon powder cook for 3 minutes
  • Stir the rice in the sauce until it is well coated and cover with a tight-fitting lid and allow to cook for about 30 minutes
  • Add the butter, sliced tomato and sliced onions stir together briefly, reduced the heat and allow it to steam up
  • Season, boil and fry the chicken
